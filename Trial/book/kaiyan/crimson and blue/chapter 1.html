Chapter One


Present day


March 15, 2024


Somewhere in Metro Manila, Philippines


I sit in my office, staring at the framed picture on my desk. We had taken the photo together, me in the middle, mum and dad on either side, hugging me tight.


After the demise of my parents, which was thirteen years ago, I moved in to uncle Max's house because he said it was not safe if I went back to live in my parent's house. I was also too young to live all alone in that house and he was afraid I would harm myself. I remember one day when I had come to live with him, I attempted to stab myself with a knife because my classmate told me I was an orphan.


I have lived with the guilt of not being able to do anything to protect my parents that day.


I would not eat, talk or even smile at anyone at home or in school. It was after a long time that I recovered by the help of uncle Max. He's like a father to me and I am lucky he came into my life.


Just as I'm lost in thought, my phone buzzes. It is uncle Max, now Police General. I cough, clearing my throat and pick up the call. "Marian, get to my office, we have a briefing." He says shortly.


I stand up and smooth out my uniform and head to his office. When I arrive at my uncle's office, Alex is already there. We've been team mates for about a year now. Alex is in the PNP Tech Sector, he's not really into the investigation stuff but good in computers that's why Uncle Max paired me up with him.


Everyone at PNP knows that Alex has a crush on me, but I have been acting oblivious to him. He's handsome, and well mannered but I don't feel that kind of pull towards him. So I've kept him at arms length.


"Alright, team. We've got a mission. There's a poker game that's been organised in a casino called Eclipse at Parañaque by an unknown gang. Intel says it's a high-stakes poker game tonight."


Myuncle hands me afile. I scan the file, taking in the details of the mission together with Alex.


"Alex go to the Automobile Department and check on your car for the ride, you'll be posing as Marian's driver. The car is almost ready," He says to Alex.


Uncle Max said that probably because, he doesn't want Alex or anyone know the main reason why he gave methe Mariano case to handle, because it could cost him his position if they discover he did that for personal gains. He wants me to avenge on them and get the justice I've longed for my parents.


"Yes sir," Alex answers and leaves. As soon as Alex leaves, uncle Max turns to me.


"Marian, I believe that this casino is been ran by the Marianos. You will pose as a VIP member looking forward to invest in the casino. Make sure your cover doesn't blow up." He says.


"And if it turns out it belongs to the Marianos, our plan remains unchanged. My mission tonight is to get involved with one of his closest guy and get him to be drawn to me." I say and Uncle Max smiles and nods.


"That's good. Alex will be providing surveillance from outside."


Your attire for tonight has been sent to the safehouse. You can leave now and get ready. Alex will join you soon." Uncle Max says and I nod, exiting his office.



PNP Safehouse, Manila


7 : 45 pm


I head to the safehouse to get ready for tonight. Alex is still at PNP, waiting for our car for tonight to be ready. As I walk in, my eyes sweep through the dimly lit living room. Uncle Max said my dress was been brought to the safehouse. And that's when I see it- the dress, it's like it belongs to a fancy celebrity who was going to Met Gala.


It's draped over a couch and I brush my fingers on the dress. I explode out of excitement picturing myself in this gown. I go to the changing room and put it on. It is a fitted, strapless black evening groll with a sweetheart neckline. The dress hugs my curves perfectly, accentuating my petite frame. The dress is a sleek, high-low design, with the front falling just above my knees and the back cascading down to myankles. I pair it with black stiletto heels, the kind that adds a deadly edge to my stride. The heels are sleek, black, and pointed, with a 4-inch spike that makes me almost look like I'm floating.


I check myself in the mirror, adjusting the wig and makeup. Damn! Who's this gorgeous lady here?


After I am done, I step outside and see Alex busy on his phone. He lifts his head up and when his eyes land on me, his eyes widen and for a moment, he's speechless. I try to hide how fluttered I am because of his attention, but my cheeks flush red.


"Hey, Alex, I didnt know you had come back from PNP." I say, trying to ward off the akward moment.


Alex clears his throat, as his expression comes back to normal. "Uh...yeah, I just arrived here," he says.


"You look stunning Mariana." He says.


"Thank you," I mumble.


"It's my pleasure," Alex says, never taking his eyes off me.


"Ready?" He asks and steps aside revealing a sleek, black Audi e-tron GT. The car is modern, with tinted windows and a sporty look. This could only be the work of the PNP Automobile Sector.


I nod seeing the car. "PNP's wheels, huh?"


"Yeah. It's... fast." Alex replies.


Alex opens the door for me, "For you milady," he says and bows. I fake a smile and sit in the car. Why is he acting all gentleman today? He's a gentleman but today, it's extreme...gentlemaness. If only there's a word like that. Alex takes the driver's seat, and we head to the warehouse where the game is being hosted.


As wedrive, I catch Alex's gaze at me through the rearview mirror and for a split second, our eyes lock onto each other. I look away quickly, smiling awkwardly. Is he checking me out? Do I really look that stunning that he can't take his eyes off me?


Throughout the drive, I pray to God silently that we reach safely without engaging in any accident because his focus is clearly not on the road. We arrive at the warehouse, and Alex parks a block away.


"Time to get to work," he says, looking at the building. I nod, and he gets out of the car and rushes to open the door for me. I step out and smile. Tonight's gonna be fun!



Somewhere in Parañaque...


Eclipse Casino


8 : 20 pm


I step into the warehouse as my heels clicking on the rough concrete floor. The air is thick with the smell of old boxes and dust. I breathe it in, letting my eyes adjust to the dim lighting. As I walk further in, I see a sprawling neon lit building. "Eclipse" is emblazoned across the front in bold displayed in LED letters.


Two massive bodyguards who look like they just walked out of an action movie are standing by the entrance. They are dressed in black suits and shades. I walk towards them struttingly.


"Business card," one of them growls.


I smile and pull out my fake ID provided by the PNP's Tech Sector. "Rosita, owner of Crescent Fashion Company," I say, trying to sound confident.


The guard takes the card and gives me a hard stare, and then nods. Alex is taking care of the systems. He's hacking through it right now.


The guard steps aside, and I walk through the door. The air is thick with smoke and it makes my skin tingle. I breathe it in, letting the atmosphere seep into my pores, because I'm gonna have a long night. I scan the room with a practiced gaze and my eyes drift over the sea of faces.


I get why they made this place like this. Outside, it looks like an abandoned building and you wouldn't even think there's any sort of thing going on here.


As I move through the crowd, the men turn to watch me, with a mixture of curiosity and desire. I was clearly the only woman in a sea of suits. I ignore them, giving a woman-of-power vibe. I have a role to play, and I will play it to perfection.


I make my way to the bar swaying my hips gently as I walk. The bartender is a lean, handsome man with a charming smile. He greets me with a nod. "Welcome to Eclipse. What can I get you, beautiful?"


I meet his gaze and I hold it for a moment before I speak. "Whiskey, neat. The best you have."


The bartender raises an eyebrow with his never wipping smile. "Coming right up."


In a few minutes, my whiskey is ready and the bartender hands it to me. I take a sip and the liquid burns its way down my throat. I savor the taste and the sensation, before turning to survey the room once more.


I see a man standing near the poker table with his eyes fixed on me with an intensity that makes my skin prickle. At first, I thought he knew who I was but with the way he is looking at me, it's my beauty charm that's working on him. I feel a flutter in my chest and smile to myself as I take another sip of my drink.


I pretend not to notice him and look somewhere else, but I could feel his eyes on me, boring into my skin. I knew I had him hooked.
