BOOK BLURB


Power doesn’t forgive weakness. And Adrian Vale has none.


When his grandfather’s will demands one impossible condition—marriage before inheritance—Adrian is forced into a corner. The empire he built his life around is slipping through his fingers, and the board is watching for any excuse to take it all.


His fiancée should have been the solution. Instead, she’s absent, distant, and unwilling to play the role he needs.


Then Lena Carter walks into the wrong boardroom.


Sharp-tongued, fearless, and entirely unimpressed by his authority, she does what no one else dares—she challenges him in front of everyone. Exposes the cracks in his leadership. And for the first time, Adrian Vale is caught off guard.


Not by fear.


By interest.


So he makes her an offer: Be his wife. Publicly. Temporarily. Strictly business.


Lena refuses—until she doesn’t.


What begins as a calculated arrangement quickly becomes something neither of them planned for. Shared spaces. Stolen glances. Lines that were never supposed to blur.


Because Lena doesn’t just play the role of his wife.


She sees the man behind the empire.


And Adrian, who has spent his life controlling everything, is about to discover the one thing he cannot control her


But in a world built on power and deception, truth doesn’t stay hidden for long.


And when it breaks loose… it won’t just destroy the contract.


It will destroy them.
